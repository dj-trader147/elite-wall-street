About Elite Wall Street

We're on a mission to democratize trading education and build the world's most supportive trading community.

Our Story

Elite Wall Street was founded in 2020 by a group of professional traders who believed that quality trading education should be accessible to everyone.

After years of working in traditional finance, our founders saw that retail traders lacked the resources and community support available to institutional traders. They set out to change that.

Today, we've grown into a community of over 5,000 active traders from around the world, united by a passion for the markets and a commitment to continuous learning.
