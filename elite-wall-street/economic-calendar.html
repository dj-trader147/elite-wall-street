Trading Tips for Economic Events

1

Check Before Trading

Always review the economic calendar before placing trades. Major events can cause sudden volatility.

2

Focus on High Impact

Red (high impact) events like Fed decisions and NFP typically move markets the most.

3

Compare Actual vs Expected

Markets react to the difference between actual results and expectations, not just the numbers.

4

Avoid Trading During News

Consider staying out during major announcements due to increased spreads and slippage.

5

Use Stop Losses

Always use stop losses when trading around economic events to protect your capital.

6

Wait for Confirmation

Initial reactions can be misleading. Wait for price to settle before making trading decisions.
